## Overview

Spaces keep your projects structured and manageable.

## How To Organize

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create spaces for each project or team">
    Use the **New Space** button. Name your space descriptively (e.g. "Marketing Docs").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation pages">
    Within a space, click **Add Page**. Structure pages by topic or workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Use the **Invite** option to add team members who need access.
  </Step>
</Steps>
