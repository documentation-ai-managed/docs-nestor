## Steps

To create a new space in Nestor:

1. Click the **"New Space"** button on your dashboard.
2. Enter a name and optional description.
3. Click **Create**.

Your new space will appear in your spaces list. Spaces help you organize project documentation by team, topic, or client.

<Callout kind="info">
You can invite collaborators by opening the space and selecting **"Invite"**.
</Callout>
