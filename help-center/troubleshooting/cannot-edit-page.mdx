---
title: Cannot Edit a Page
description: Trouble editing pages in Nestor? Try these quick fixes.
---

## Symptoms
- Edit button is disabled
- Changes aren't saved

## Possible Causes
- Insufficient permissions
- Page is locked by another user

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your permissions">
    Make sure you have Editor access to the space. Ask the owner if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh or try later">
    The page may be locked for editing. Wait a few minutes and retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
